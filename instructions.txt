1. The objective of the game is to fly the furthest away from Planet Sully
2. To navigate the ship, you use the arrow keys and have to constantly move forward
3. The farther you get, the higher your score
4. If your ship touches the asteroids, your ship takes damage
5. Your only saviors are the Sully-droids which will will bring your slush fund back to 100%
6. You die and the game ends when the slush fund gets to 0
